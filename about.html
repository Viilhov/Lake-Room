About Lake Rooms

Welcome to our relaxing haven in the heart of Iznajar!
I'm Barbera, and this is my furry companion, Jakey. We're absolutely thrilled to have you join us.
We're delighted to offer two exquisite double rooms, each granting you a panoramic view of the tranquil lake that's nothing short of breathtaking. Wake up to the sun's gentle caress painting the water with hues of gold, and let the tranquil ripples lull you into serenity.
Beyond the comfort of our home, a world of charm awaits you. The heart of Iznajar beckons, with its cobbled streets and beautiful castle. And when you return from your adventures, Jakey and our welcoming atmosphere will make you feel right at home. We can't wait to meet you, share stories, and ensure your time here is absolutely unforgettable.
Your lakeside escape awaits!
